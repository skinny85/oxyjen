Oxyjen (pronounced 'oxygen') is a lightweight, easy to use and powerful templating engine and code generation tool. It enables quick and boilerplate-less early development while giving you access to the full power of a programming language should you need it. With OxyjenZone, a free online public repository service, you have thousands of ready-made templates at your disposal, and can effortlessly share your own.

Easy install

Installing Oxyjen is incredibly easy. Just download the latest release, unzip it anywhere on your computer, and you're done! You can optionally add it to your system's PATH for convenience. Detailed instructions are given below.

Built for usability and power

Oxyjen was built from the ground up to be easy to use. However, that does not take away anything from its power. By leveraging JavaScript, a fully-fledged programming language, it makes easy things almost trivial, and hard things possible. Learn how easy it is to use.
